Hey, let's play 9x9 Tic-Tac-Toe.

Rules are simple:
- There is a big board with 9 small boards in each box.
- Win the small board to win and mark the box yours
- The first move you can play in any board, but then the opponent sends you to specific boards.
- For example, if player 1 plays in the bottom left corner of a small board, player 2 must play their move in bottom left corner of the big board
- If small board is marked by a player or tied/out-of-moves, then being send to that box gives you chance to play anywhere.

For a better discribe rules, please refer to google.
To play please go to home or click 'Okie' on the top left corner.
